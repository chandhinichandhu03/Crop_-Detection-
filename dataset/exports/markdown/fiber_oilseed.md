### 🌾 Crop Cultivation & Agronomy Guide

- **Origin**: Mesoamerica (Mexico-Central America region)
- **Climatic Preference**: Warm tropical and subtropical (Temp: 21-30°C; minimum 15°C for germination, Rainfall: 500-1500mm; distributed throughout growing season)
- **Soil Requirements**: Deep loamy or clay-loam 'black cotton soil' (vertisol) preferred (pH: 5.5-7.0)

### 🦠 Pathological Diagnosis (Diseases)

#### Cotton Leaf Curl Virus Disease (CLCuD) (Viral)
- **Pathogen**: *Cotton leaf curl Multan virus (CLCuMV) + betasatellite*
- **Symptoms**: Upward curling of leaves, vein thickening, enations (leaf-like outgrowths) on underside of leaves
- **Treatment Protocols**:
  * **Organic**: Control whiteflies with neem extract, reflective mulch, remove infected plants
  * **Chemical**: Imidacloprid/Thiamethoxam soil application for whitefly control; Acetamiprid foliar spray
  * **Prevention**: Resistant varieties (NIAB 846, CIM-499), control whiteflies strictly, remove infected plants, use insect-proof nets in nursery

### 🐛 Entomological Threats (Pests)

#### Mealybugs (*Phenacoccus solenopsis (Cotton Mealybug); Planococcus citri (Citrus Mealybug)*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Yellowing, leaf drop, honeydew + sooty mold; cotton twisting; mango malformation spread; overall plant decline and death in severe infestations
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Neem oil + soap spray; rubbing alcohol on cotton swabs; strong water spray; remove waxy coating first; Cryptolaemus montrouzieri release
  * **Chemical**: Imidacloprid soil drench (2ml/L), Buprofezin 25% WP (1g/L), Chlorpyrifos 20% EC (2.5ml/L), Acetamiprid + Chlorpyrifos
  * **Biological**: Cryptolaemus montrouzieri (mealybug destroyer beetle), Leptomastix dactylopii (parasitoid wasp), Anagyrus kamali, Acerophagus papayae

#### Thrips (*Thrips palmi (Melon Thrips); Frankliniella occidentalis (Western Flower Thrips)*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Silvery streaks/scarring on leaves and fruits (from feeding); distorted growth; 'ghost spots' on petals; TSWV virus symptoms (wilting, bronzing, ring spots)
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Blue sticky traps (most attractive to thrips), spinosad spray, neem oil, kaolin clay, predatory mites (Neoseiulus cucumeris)
  * **Chemical**: Spinosad 45% SC (0.3ml/L), Imidacloprid, Abamectin 1.9% EC (0.5ml/L), Cyantraniliprole, Tolfenpyrad
  * **Biological**: Neoseiulus cucumeris, Amblyseius swirskii (predatory mites); Orius insidiosus (pirate bug); Steinernema feltiae (entomopathogenic nematode) for soil pupae

#### Whitefly (*Bemisia tabaci*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Yellowing leaves, sticky honeydew, sooty mold (secondary), vector of 200+ plant viruses (TYLCV, CLCuD, CBSD, cassava mosaic)
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Yellow sticky traps, neem oil + soap spray (5ml+2ml per L), reflective mulch, soap water spray, Beauveria bassiana
  * **Chemical**: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL (0.5ml/L), Thiamethoxam 25% WG (0.2g/L), Acetamiprid 20% SP (0.2g/L), Spiromesifen (resistance management)
  * **Biological**: Encarsia formosa (parasitic wasp), Eretmocerus mundus, Macrolophus pygmaeus (predatory bug), Beauveria bassiana

#### Cotton Bollworm (Helicoverpa) (*Helicoverpa armigera*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Entry holes in cotton bolls (with frass-filled tunnels), tomato fruit damage, chickpea pod damage with grain loss, maize kernel damage
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: NPV spray (2×10¹² POB/ha); HaNPV + cotton Bt; pheromone traps (1/ha); neem seed kernel extract; Bt-k spray
  * **Chemical**: Emamectin benzoate 5% SG (0.4g/L), Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC (0.3ml/L), Indoxacarb, Flubendiamide, Thiodicarb
  * **Biological**: Trichogramma chilonis (egg parasitoid, 1 lakh/ha weekly), Habrobracon hebetor, Chrysoperla carnea, HaNPV

#### Spider Mite (Two-Spotted) (*Tetranychus urticae*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Stippled (tiny pale dots/punctures) on leaves from below; leaves turn bronze/yellow and dry; fine silk webbing; premature defoliation
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Water spray to wash off; neem oil 5ml/L; wettable sulfur 3g/L; insecticidal soap; predatory mites release; soap solution sprays
  * **Chemical**: Abamectin 1.9% EC (0.5ml/L), Spiromesifen 22.9% SC (1ml/L), Hexythiazox 5% EC (1ml/L), Bifenazate, Clofentezine, Fenpyroximate
  * **Biological**: Phytoseiulus persimilis (excellent predator), Amblyseius californicus, Neoseiulus californicus, Feltiella acarisuga (gall midge)

#### Leafhoppers (*Amrasca biguttula biguttula (Cotton/Okra Leafhopper); Empoasca spp.*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Cotton: 'Cotton leaf curl' or 'Jassid damage' — leaf edge curling upward, reddening; Potato/Brinjal: leaf hopper burn (hopperburn) — marginal leaf scorch and curling
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Neem seed kernel extract 5%, neem oil; yellow sticky traps; Beauveria bassiana spray
  * **Chemical**: Imidacloprid 17.8% SL (0.3ml/L), Lambda-cyhalothrin 5% EC (1ml/L), Thiamethoxam, Dimethoate 30% EC (2ml/L)
  * **Biological**: Anagrus atomus (egg parasitoid in grapes), Gonatocerus ashmeadi, Stethorus ladybugs

#### Fall Armyworm (*Spodoptera frugiperda*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Ragged windows in leaves; characteristic 'shot hole' damage in whorl; frass in leaf whorl; direct ear damage (in maize); can completely defoliate young plants
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Bt-kurstaki or Bt-aizawai spray; fall armyworm pheromone traps; sand+soil in whorl (suffocates larvae); egg mass and larvae hand collection
  * **Chemical**: Emamectin benzoate 5% SG (0.4g/L into whorl), Chlorantraniliprole (Coragen), Spinetoram, Lambda-cyhalothrin (adult moths)
  * **Biological**: Telenomus remus (egg parasitoid), Cotesia icipe, Coccygomimus turionellae, Metarhizium rileyi, NPV (Nuclear Polyhedrosis Virus)

#### Root Knot Nematode (*Meloidogyne incognita, M. javanica, M. arenaria, M. hapla*)
- **Damage Symptoms**: Above ground: wilting, chlorosis, stunting resembling nutrient deficiency; Below ground: characteristic root galls (knots), reduced and brown root system, secondary root pathogens
- **IPM & Control Measures**:
  * **Organic/IPM**: Marigold (Tagetes spp.) intercropping; neem cake 250kg/ha soil application; Paecilomyces lilacinus inoculant; castor cake; mustard cake; crop rotation with cereals
  * **Chemical**: Carbofuran 3G (33 kg/ha), Phorate 10G, Ethoprophos 10G at planting; soil fumigation with Dazomet or Metam sodium (pre-plant)
  * **Biological**: Purpureocillium lilacinum (formerly Paecilomyces lilacinus), Pochonia chlamydosporia, Trichoderma harzianum, Bacillus firmus (VOTiVO), Steinernema carpocapsae
